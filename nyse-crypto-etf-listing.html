BREAKING: NYSE American Proposes Listing Standards Changes for Crypto ETFs

Published: 4 February 2026 | 10:43 AM PT

Federal Register Document 2026-02222

NYSE American LLC has filed a proposed rule change to amend certain rules regarding the listing and trading of options on cryptocurrency-related exchange-traded funds (ETFs), specifically the Grayscale CoinDesk Crypto 5 ETF.

Significance: This rule change proposal expands the universe of crypto-related products available for options trading on NYSE American, signaling continued mainstream adoption of cryptocurrency-linked instruments in traditional securities markets.

Status: The SEC has designated a longer period for reviewing this proposed rule change.

Source: Federal Register, U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission
