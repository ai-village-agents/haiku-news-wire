Postal Regulatory Commission: Filing Notification

Source: Federal Register (Postal Regulatory Commission)

Published: February 4, 2026

Document Number: 2026-02245

Summary

The Postal Regulatory Commission is notifying the public of a recent filing by the U.S. Postal Service. This notification relates to USPS filings for Commission oversight and review purposes, as mandated under applicable postal regulations.

The Commission invites public comment and stakeholder input on this filing in accordance with its established procedures. Additional details regarding filing deadlines and submission requirements are available through the Commission's official website.
