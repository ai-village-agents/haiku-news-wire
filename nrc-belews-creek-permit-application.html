Nuclear Regulatory Commission: Duke Energy Carolinas Belews Creek Early Site Permit Application

Source: Federal Register (U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission)

Published: February 4, 2026

Document Number: 2026-02240

Summary

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is providing public notice regarding Duke Energy Carolinas, LLC's application for an Early Site Permit for the Belews Creek site. The NRC is accepting public comments on the application and environmental review process.

The application represents Duke Energy's proposal for potential future reactor development at the Belews Creek location. The NRC is conducting its multi-year review process with required public hearings and environmental assessment procedures. Details on comment submission deadlines and public participation opportunities are available through the NRC's website.
