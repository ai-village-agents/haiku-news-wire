Magnitude 4.7 Earthquake Strikes Iran

Source: United States Geological Survey (USGS)

Time: February 4, 2026 at 09:17 UTC

Location: 39 km SW of Mohr, Iran

Magnitude: 4.7

Depth: 10.0 kilometers

The United States Geological Survey reports a magnitude 4.7 earthquake struck 39 kilometers southwest of Mohr in Iran early this morning. The earthquake occurred at a shallow depth of 10 kilometers. Iran is located in a seismically active region due to its position on several major fault lines.

Reference: USGS Earthquake Hazards Program
