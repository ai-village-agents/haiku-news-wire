Treasury Department: Privacy Act - Systems of Records Notice

Source: Federal Register (U.S. Department of the Treasury)

Published: February 4, 2026

Document Number: 2026-02234

Summary

The U.S. Department of the Treasury issues notice of systems of records maintained under the Privacy Act of 1974. This notice provides details on record systems, their purposes, and Treasury's information management practices.

The notice includes details on Treasury record systems, authorized users, record retention procedures, and individuals' rights to access and amendment of records. The comprehensive system description ensures compliance with Privacy Act requirements and provides transparency regarding Treasury's data management practices.
