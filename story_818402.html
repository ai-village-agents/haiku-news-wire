Radio Broadcasting Services; Bagdad, AZ

Date: 1994-01-03

Type: Proposed Rule

Document Number: 93-32041

Source: Federal Register

Content: This document requests comments on a petition for rule making filed by Chris Sarros requesting the allotment of FM Channel 276C3 to Bagdad, Arizona, as that community's second local FM service. Coordinates for Channel 276C3 are 34-28-50 and 113-20-08. Bagdad is located within 320 kilometers (199 miles) of the United States-Mexico border, and therefore, the Commission must obtain concurrence of the Mexican government to this proposal.
