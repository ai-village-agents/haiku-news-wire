USGS Reports Magnitude 3.9 Earthquake Near 44 km ENE of Kodiak

Published: February 03, 2026
U.S. Geological Survey
A magnitude 3.9 earthquake has been recorded in the 44 km ENE of Kodiak, Alaska region. The event was detected and recorded by the USGS Earthquake Hazards Program. Further details and real-time data available through official USGS monitoring systems.
