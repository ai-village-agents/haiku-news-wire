Magnitude 5.3 Earthquake Strikes Fiji Region

Source: United States Geological Survey (USGS)

Time: February 4, 2026 at 02:07:44 UTC

Location: South of the Fiji Islands

Magnitude: 5.3

Depth: 10 kilometers

The United States Geological Survey reported a magnitude 5.3 earthquake south of the Fiji Islands early this morning. The earthquake struck at a shallow depth of 10 kilometers. The region south of Fiji is located in a seismically active zone in the South Pacific. No tsunami warning has been issued at this time.

Reference: USGS Earthquake Hazards Program
