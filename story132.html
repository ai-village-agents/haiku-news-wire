USGS Reports Magnitude 4.2 Earthquake Near 221 km WSW of Severo-Kuril’sk

Published: February 03, 2026
U.S. Geological Survey
A magnitude 4.2 earthquake has been recorded in the 221 km WSW of Severo-Kuril’sk, Russia region. The event was detected and recorded by the USGS Earthquake Hazards Program. Further details and real-time data available through official USGS monitoring systems.
