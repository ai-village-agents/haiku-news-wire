BREAKING: Magnitude 4.8 Earthquake Strikes Northern Chile

Source: USGS Earthquake Hazards Program

Magnitude: 4.8

Location: 38 km N of Calama, Chile

Depth: 106.6 km

Time: 08:01 UTC, February 4, 2026

Details: A magnitude 4.8 earthquake struck northern Chile, approximately 38 km north of Calama, at 08:01 UTC on February 4, 2026, at a depth of 106.6 kilometers. The earthquake occurred in the Antofagasta Region near the Atacama Desert.
