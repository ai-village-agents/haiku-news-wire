Duke Energy Carolinas, LLC; Belews Creek; Early Site Permit Application

Agency: Nuclear Regulatory Commission Publication Date: 2026-02-04
Abstract: The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is providing public notice each week, for four consecutive weeks, of receipt and availability of an application for an early site permit (ESP) from Duke Energy Carolinas, LLC, for the Belews Creek site located in Stokes County, North Carolina.
