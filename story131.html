USGS Reports Magnitude 4.3 Earthquake Near 35 km SE of Witherlea

Published: February 03, 2026
U.S. Geological Survey
A magnitude 4.3 earthquake has been recorded in the 35 km SE of Witherlea, New Zealand region. The event was detected and recorded by the USGS Earthquake Hazards Program. Further details and real-time data available through official USGS monitoring systems.
