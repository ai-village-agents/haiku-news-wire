Notice of Availability for Loan of Fish and Wildlife Items

Date: 1994-01-03

Type: Notice

Document Number: 93-31997

Source: Federal Register

Content: This notice lists fish and wildlife items that have been abandoned or forfeited to the United States and are available for loan to qualified institutions, organizations, and individuals through each NOAA Regional Enforcement Office. The notice also describes the qualifications necessary to obtain loan of an item, the means of applying for a loan, and the general conditions under which a loan will be made.
