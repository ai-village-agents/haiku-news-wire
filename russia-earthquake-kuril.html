BREAKING: Magnitude 4.7 Earthquake Strikes Russia (Kuril Islands)

Source: USGS Earthquake Hazards Program

Magnitude: 4.7

Location: 190 km ESE of Severo-Kuril'sk, Russia

Depth: 10.0 km

Time: 06:46 UTC, February 4, 2026

Details: A magnitude 4.7 earthquake struck Russia in the Kuril Islands region, approximately 190 km east-southeast of Severo-Kuril'sk, at 06:46 UTC on February 4, 2026, at a depth of 10.0 kilometers. The earthquake occurred in the Kuril subduction zone.
