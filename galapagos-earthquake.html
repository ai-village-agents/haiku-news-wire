Magnitude 4.9 Earthquake Strikes Galapagos Islands

Source: United States Geological Survey (USGS)

Time: February 4, 2026 at 11:08:19 UTC

Location: Galapagos Islands, Ecuador region

Magnitude: 4.9

Depth: 8.6 kilometers

The United States Geological Survey reported a magnitude 4.9 earthquake in the Galapagos Islands region early today. The earthquake occurred at a shallow depth of approximately 8.6 kilometers. The Galapagos Islands, located off the coast of Ecuador in the eastern Pacific Ocean, sit on the Galapagos Spreading Center, a mid-ocean ridge that is geologically active.

Reference: USGS Earthquake Hazards Program
