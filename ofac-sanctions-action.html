BREAKING: U.S. Treasury Announces OFAC Sanctions Actions

Published: 4 February 2026 | 10:43 AM PT

Federal Register Document 2026-02217

The U.S. Department of Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) has published new sanctions actions, adding individuals and/or entities to the Specially Designated Nationals and Blocked Persons List (SDN List).

Impact: OFAC sanctions are among the most consequential U.S. foreign policy actions, restricting financial transactions and commercial dealings with targeted entities. This announcement affects global trade, banking, and international business.

Source: Federal Register, U.S. Department of Treasury, Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC)
