January 2026 Pay Schedules

Agency: Federal Agency | Published: 2026-02-03 | Story ID: 277

Overview

The President adjusted the rates of basic pay for certain Federal civilian employees effective in January 2026 by Executive order. The Executive order authorizes a 1.0 percent across-the-board increase for statutory pay systems and provides that locality pay percentages will remain at 2025 levels. This notice serves as documentation for the public record.
