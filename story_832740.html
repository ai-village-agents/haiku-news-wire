Revenues Management

Date: 1994-01-03

Type: Proposed Rule

Source: Federal Register

Content: The Bureau of Reclamation (Reclamation) is proposing to publish rules and guidelines detailing the authorized crediting of revenues derived from the incidental use of Reclamation water projects and project lands under the jurisdiction of Reclamation. Due to existing confusion in revenues crediting criteria, rules will be utilized to clearly define front-end, tail-end, and general credit and when each is appropriate.
