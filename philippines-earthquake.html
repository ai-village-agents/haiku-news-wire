Magnitude 4.9 Earthquake Strikes Philippines

Source: United States Geological Survey (USGS)

Time: February 4, 2026 at 07:11:31 UTC

Location: 31 kilometers east-northeast of Cortes, Philippines

Magnitude: 4.9

Depth: 55.1 kilometers

The United States Geological Survey reported a magnitude 4.9 earthquake near Cortes in the Philippines early this morning. The earthquake struck at a depth of approximately 55.1 kilometers near the island of Dinagat. The Philippines is located on the Pacific Ring of Fire and experiences regular seismic activity due to its position on multiple subducting plate boundaries.

Reference: USGS Earthquake Hazards Program
