Transportation Department: Request for Information on National Strategy for Transportation Digital Infrastructure

Source: Federal Register (U.S. Department of Transportation)

Published: February 4, 2026

Document Number: 2026-02236

Summary

The U.S. Department of Transportation's Office of the Resistant Secretary for Research and Technology is requesting public input on research priorities and strategies to support the establishment of a comprehensive national strategy for transportation digital infrastructure.

This initiative aims to identify critical research needs, coordinate federal and private sector efforts, and develop policy frameworks for next-generation transportation technology systems. Public comments are invited from industry, research institutions, and other stakeholders.
