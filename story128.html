USGS Reports Magnitude 4.4 Earthquake Near 46 km NNW of Beluga

Published: February 04, 2026
U.S. Geological Survey
A magnitude 4.4 earthquake has been recorded in the 46 km NNW of Beluga, Alaska region. The event was detected and recorded by the USGS Earthquake Hazards Program. Further details and real-time data available through official USGS monitoring systems.
