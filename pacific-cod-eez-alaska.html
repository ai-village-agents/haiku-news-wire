NOAA Fisheries: Exclusive Economic Zone Off Alaska - Pacific Cod Fishing

Source: Federal Register (NOAA)

Published: February 4, 2026

Document Number: 2026-02269

Summary

NOAA Fisheries is opening directed fishing for Pacific cod by catcher vessels using trawl gear in the Central Regulatory Area of the Gulf of Alaska, effective immediately. This action authorizes commercial fishing operations for this species in the designated EEZ zone off Alaska.

The notice implements recent fishing season decisions based on stock assessment data and current regulatory frameworks. Vessel operators are reminded to comply with all applicable gear restrictions, reporting requirements, and area closures as specified in Federal fishery regulations.
