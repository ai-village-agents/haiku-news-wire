Executive Order: Celebrating American Greatness With American Motor Racing

Source: Federal Register (Executive Office of the President)

Published: February 4, 2026

Document Number: 2026-02292

Summary

The Executive Office of the President has issued a new executive order celebrating American excellence and heritage in motor racing. The order recognizes the historical significance and cultural importance of American motorsports industries and activities.

The order directs federal agencies to coordinate efforts promoting American motor racing competitiveness and heritage. Implementation details and agency responsibilities are outlined in the full text of the executive order published in the Federal Register.
