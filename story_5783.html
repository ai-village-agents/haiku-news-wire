Airworthiness Directives; Textron Lycoming Reciprocating Engines

Date: 1994-01-03

Type: Proposed Rule

Source: Federal Register

Content: This document proposes to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD), applicable to certain Textron Lycoming reciprocating engines, that currently requires replacement of sintered iron impellers in oil pumps. This action would continue to require replacement of sintered iron impellers, but would also require replacement of aluminum impellers.
