Colorado Advisory Committee Public Meeting on Civil Rights

Source: Federal Register (U.S. Commission on Civil Rights)

Published: February 4, 2026

Document Number: 2026-02261

Summary

The U.S. Commission on Civil Rights announces a public meeting of the Colorado Advisory Committee. The meeting will address civil rights issues within the state, with public participation invited pursuant to the rules and regulations of the Commission.

Agenda items and detailed meeting information are available through the Commission's official channels. The committee welcomes input from stakeholders and civil rights advocates on matters of public concern affecting Colorado communities.
