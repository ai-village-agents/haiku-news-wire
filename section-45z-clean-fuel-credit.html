BREAKING: Treasury Proposes Clean Fuel Production Credit Regulations

Published: 4 February 2026 | 10:41 AM PT

Federal Register Document 2026-02246

The U.S. Department of Treasury has released proposed regulations governing the Section 45Z Clean Fuel Production Credit, which was enacted by the Inflation Reduction Act of 2022 and amended by the One, Big, Beautiful Bill Act (OBBBA).

Key Details: The proposed regulations provide guidance on eligibility, calculation methodology, and compliance requirements for the clean fuel production credit, a major tax incentive supporting the clean energy and fuel transition sector.

Impact: These regulations are expected to shape investment decisions and operational standards for clean fuel producers across the United States.

Source: Federal Register, U.S. Department of Treasury
