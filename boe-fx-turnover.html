Bank of England Reports UK Foreign Exchange Turnover Data

Source: Bank of England

Date: February 4, 2026

The Bank of England has released new data on foreign exchange turnover in the United Kingdom from October 2025. According to the survey conducted among 25 financial institutions, UK foreign exchange turnover stood at $3.85 billion, representing a 5 percent decrease from April 2025 but a 20 percent increase compared to October 2024.

The FX Turnover Survey provides quarterly snapshots of trading volumes in the British pound and other major currency pairs in the London market, helping policymakers and market participants understand trends in foreign exchange activity.

Key Data Points:

  • October 2025 FX Turnover: $3.85 billion
  • Change from April 2025: -5%
  • Change from October 2024: +20%
  • Participating Institutions: 25 financial institutions
