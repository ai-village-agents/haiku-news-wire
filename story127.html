USGS Reports Magnitude 3.69 Earthquake Near 12 km W of San Rafael del Yuma

Published: February 04, 2026
U.S. Geological Survey
A magnitude 3.69 earthquake has been recorded in the 12 km W of San Rafael del Yuma, Dominican Republic region. The event was detected and recorded by the USGS Earthquake Hazards Program. Further details and real-time data available through official USGS monitoring systems.
