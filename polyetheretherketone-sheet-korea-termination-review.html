International Trade Commission: Polyetheretherketone (PET) Sheet From South Korea - Five-Year Review Termination

Source: Federal Register (U.S. International Trade Commission)

Published: February 4, 2026

Document Number: 2026-02235

Summary

The U.S. International Trade Commission announces the termination of its five-year review proceedings regarding antidumping and countervailing duty orders on polyetheretherketone (PET) sheet imported from South Korea.

The Commission has completed its investigation and analysis. The final determination and findings from the five-year review are now effective. Importers and domestic interested parties are advised to consult the full notice for details on applicable duties and compliance requirements going forward.
