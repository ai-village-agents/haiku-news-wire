BREAKING: NOAA Closes Commercial Harvest for Spanish Mackerel in Atlantic Southern Zone

Published: 4 February 2026 | 10:44 AM PT

Federal Register Document 2026-02275

The National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS), part of NOAA, has closed commercial harvest for the Atlantic migratory group of Spanish mackerel in the southern zone, effective immediately.

Reason: NMFS projects that landings of Spanish mackerel will soon reach the commercial quota for the Atlantic southern zone in the 2025-2026 fishing year. This closure is necessary to protect the Spanish mackerel resource in the Atlantic.

Impact: The closure restricts commercial fishing activity in the Atlantic region and affects the fishing industry in the southern Atlantic zone.

Source: Federal Register, NOAA National Marine Fisheries Service
