BREAKING: U.S. Removes Cambodia From Export Embargo List

Published: 4 February 2026 | 10:41 AM PT

Federal Register Document 2026-02262

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) has issued a final rule removing Cambodia from the Country Group D:5 designation under the Export Administration Regulations (EAR). This change reflects Cambodia's removal as an arms embargoed destination under the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR), following a determination made by the Secretary of State on November 7, 2025.

Key Impact: The removal will affect export controls and arms restrictions that previously applied to Cambodia, signaling a change in U.S. policy toward the Southeast Asian nation.

Source: Federal Register, U.S. Department of Commerce, Bureau of Industry and Security
