National Medal of Technology Nomination Evaluation Committee; Meeting

Date: 1994-01-03

Type: Notice

Source: Federal Register

Content: This notice announces the forthcoming closed meeting of the National Medal of Technology Nomination Evaluation Committee. The Committee was rechartered on September 15, 1993. The Committee shall make recommendations to the Secretary of Commerce, through a Steering Committee, concerning award of the National Medal of Technology. The Committee will meet only in executive session to discuss matters concerned with applying the criteria for determining the relative merits of all persons and companies
